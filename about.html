We're the IT team you wish you had.

IT Hub was started in 2011 by a team of sysadmins and engineers who were tired of seeing small and mid-size businesses get burned by bad IT. We're now 240 people across 6 countries — and still accountable to the same standard.

Our story

Started by operators. Built for operators.

Before IT Hub, our founders ran IT for hospitals, banks, and manufacturers. They watched the same things go wrong, over and over: vendors who oversold and under-delivered, tickets that took days, projects that came in late and over budget, and security "solutions" that were really just a logo on a slide.

So they built the firm they wished they'd had on the other side of the table. One that picks up the phone. One that documents what it does. One that treats uptime, security, and customer experience as the baseline — not the upsell.

15 years later, that's still the company. We've grown, but we haven't drifted.

What we believe

Six principles, no compromises.

01

Pick up the phone.

Real people, fast answers, 24/7. No "we'll get back to you in 48 hours". Ever.

02

Document everything.

If it isn't written down, it didn't happen. Every change, every config, every decision.

03

Under-promise, over-deliver.

Set realistic expectations and beat them. Every time.

04

Tell the truth, even when it's hard.

We'll tell you when something's a bad idea. Then we'll help you do it anyway if you insist.

05

Security is the floor, not the ceiling.

It comes baked in. We don't sell "security" as an add-on — that defeats the point.

06

Hire adults, treat them like adults.

No micromanagement, no clock-watching. Smart people do great work when you let them.
