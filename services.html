The complete IT services stack.

From the help desk to the cloud to custom software — everything your business needs to run, scale, and stay secure, delivered by one accountable partner.

01 / Managed IT Services

Your IT department, fully managed.

We become your IT team — or extend the one you have. From day-to-day support to long-term planning, we handle the operations so your people can focus on the business.

Every engagement starts with a discovery audit, a documented baseline, and a 90-day roadmap. Then we run.

  • Proactive 24/7 monitoring of servers, endpoints, and networks
  • Patch management and OS / application updates
  • Endpoint management (Windows, macOS, mobile)
  • User onboarding and offboarding
  • IT documentation and asset inventory
  • Quarterly business reviews and roadmap
  • Vendor management and license tracking
  • Hardware procurement and lifecycle
02 / Cybersecurity

Defense in depth, run by humans.

Most breaches start with something basic that nobody was watching. We close those gaps, then layer in enterprise-grade detection and response — backed by a real SOC team, not just a dashboard.

You'll get a security program that's actually appropriate for your business — and clear, plain-English reports your leadership can act on.

  • 24/7 managed SOC and threat monitoring
  • Endpoint Detection & Response (EDR / XDR)
  • Managed firewall and intrusion prevention
  • Email security and anti-phishing
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) rollout
  • Vulnerability scans and patch prioritization
  • Security awareness training and phishing sims
  • Compliance: SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, PCI-DSS, GDPR
03 / Cloud Services

Move to the cloud. Stay in control of cost.

Whether you're lifting a workload, rebuilding native, or optimizing what you already have — we architect for resilience and predictable spend. No surprise bills.

We work across AWS, Azure, GCP, and hybrid environments, and we don't play favorites.

  • Cloud migration strategy and execution
  • Multi-cloud and hybrid architecture
  • Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace admin
  • Cost optimization (FinOps) — typical 20–40% savings
  • Cloud security and identity management
  • Backup, snapshot, and disaster recovery
  • Reserved-instance and commitment planning
  • Right-sizing, tagging, and governance
04 / Custom Software Development

Software built around how you actually work.

Off-the-shelf software forces your business to fit the tool. We build the other way around — custom web apps, internal tools, and enterprise systems designed for your workflows, your data, and your scale.

Senior engineers, modern stacks, weekly demos, and a product manager who actually answers email.

  • Custom web applications (React, Vue, Angular)
  • Backend services and APIs (Node, Python, Go, .NET)
  • Enterprise integrations (SAP, Salesforce, Workday)
  • Legacy modernization and re-platforming
  • Internal tools and admin dashboards
  • SaaS product development
  • Database design and migration
  • Ongoing maintenance and enhancement retainers
05 / Web & Mobile App Development

Customer-facing apps that don't fall over on Black Friday.

Marketing websites, e-commerce storefronts, native mobile apps — built to convert and built to scale. We handle design, engineering, app store submission, and post-launch iteration.

  • Responsive websites and landing pages
  • Headless CMS and content platforms
  • E-commerce (Shopify, BigCommerce, custom)
  • Native iOS and Android apps (Swift, Kotlin)
  • Cross-platform apps (Flutter, React Native)
  • App Store optimization and submission
  • Performance, SEO, and Core Web Vitals
  • Post-launch support and iteration
06 / Data & AI / Business Intelligence

Turn your data into decisions, not dashboards.

We build the pipelines, the warehouses, and the AI models — and we tie them to the business questions you actually need to answer. Less "data lake nobody uses", more "answer in 30 seconds".

  • Data warehousing (Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift)
  • ETL / ELT pipelines (Airflow, dbt, Fivetran)
  • Dashboards and BI (Looker, Power BI, Tableau)
  • Predictive analytics and ML modeling
  • Generative AI and LLM-powered apps
  • Real-time analytics and event streaming
  • Data governance, lineage, and quality
  • Self-service analytics enablement
07 / DevOps & Automation

Ship faster. Sleep better.

We build CI/CD, infrastructure-as-code, and observability so your team deploys dozens of times a day with confidence. No more "it works on my machine" or 3 a.m. pages from on-call.

  • CI/CD pipelines (GitHub Actions, GitLab, Jenkins)
  • Infrastructure as Code (Terraform, Pulumi)
  • Kubernetes and container orchestration
  • Observability (Datadog, Grafana, Prometheus)
  • Site Reliability Engineering (SRE)
  • Automated testing and quality gates
  • Zero-downtime deployment strategies
  • Cost and performance optimization

Roadmaps, not slide decks.
