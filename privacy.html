Privacy Policy

This is a placeholder policy for the IT Hub demo website. For a real deployment, replace this with a properly drafted privacy policy reviewed by your legal team. Below is a structural template you can adapt.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly (name, email, company, messages you send us), information collected automatically (IP, browser, pages visited), and information from third parties (analytics, payment processors).

2. How we use your information

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To respond to your inquiries and fulfill requests
  • To send you service updates, security alerts, and (with consent) marketing
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and prevent abuse

3. Legal bases (EEA / UK)

If you are in the EEA or UK, we process your data under one or more of: performance of a contract, our legitimate interests (operating a secure service), your consent, or legal obligation.

4. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with: service providers who help us operate (hosting, email, analytics), legal authorities when required, and in connection with a business transfer (acquisition, merger).

5. Data retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary for the purposes described, or as required by law. Marketing data is deleted within 30 days of opt-out.

6. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, export, or restrict processing of your personal data, and to object to processing. Email privacy@ithub.com to exercise these rights. We respond within 30 days.

7. Security

We maintain administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. We are SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certified.

8. International transfers

When we transfer data from the EEA, UK, or Switzerland to other countries, we use Standard Contractual Clauses or other approved mechanisms.

9. Children

Our services are not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

10. Cookies and similar technologies

We use first-party cookies for essential site functionality, and (with consent) third-party cookies for analytics. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings or our cookie banner.

11. Changes to this policy

We will post any changes here and update the "Last updated" date. For material changes, we will provide additional notice (e.g., email).
