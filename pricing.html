FAQ

Common questions

Is there a setup or onboarding fee?

For Starter and Business plans, no. Enterprise plans may include a one-time setup fee depending on complexity — we'll quote it upfront in writing.

How fast can you onboard us?

Most teams are fully onboarded within 14 business days. Complex environments (200+ endpoints, multi-site, custom compliance) typically take 4–6 weeks. We document the timeline in your Statement of Work before we start.

What if we already have an internal IT team?

Perfect — we'll work alongside them. Most of our Business and Enterprise clients have 1–3 internal IT people, and we extend their capacity with our 24/7 help desk, security operations, and specialized skills.

Do you replace our current vendors?

Only if you want us to. We'll audit your existing stack, identify what to keep and what to retire, and handle the transition. We don't force proprietary tooling.

Can we pay annually?

Yes — annual billing gets you two months free. Just ask your account manager or mention it in the contact form.

What if we want to cancel?

Month-to-month plans can be canceled with 30 days' notice. Annual plans can be canceled at the renewal date. We don't believe in locking people in — we earn the renewal every year.

Do you sign NDAs and BAAs?

Yes. We routinely sign mutual NDAs and Business Associate Agreements for healthcare clients. Standard contracts are available for review before any commitment.
